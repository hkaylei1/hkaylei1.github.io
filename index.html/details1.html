Design Details

Baseball Card


This project was made in my freshman year of college, and was a very difficult process. I initially turned it in on a limited schedule, and it was a work that I was not proud of. The project was to create a baseball-style card of a designer of the professor's choice. However, when I started my portfolio to enter the BFA graphic design program, I decided to remake this and see how my style has changed. I am very proud of the result, and it was something I was not ashamed to submit. The red and pinks, along with the multi-colored border, have become a crucial part of my style, and it all started with creating this baseball card!
