Design Details

Helping Fins


This project was a very challenging yet important project for me. I am very passionate about environmental work and the preservation of the shark population. This was the first time I used Figma, and the first time I made a design project that was so in-depth that it lasted the entire semester. Learning Figma was challenging, but my partner and I worked through it together and made a concept we are both extremely proud of. I created the design guide and most of the illustrations in Adobe Illustrator and transferred them to the presentation.
