ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Kaylei Harrison. Welcome to my portfolio! I am so excited to share my work with you. I am currently a graphic design student at Central Michigan University.


I am from Trenton, Michigan, and have lived in Metro Detroit my whole life! I am the second oldest of eight siblings, and my youngest brother is 18 years younger than I am. I enjoy nature, art, and spending time exploring cities and new locations.


I have a cat named Rueben, who is the sweetest and most rambunctious kitten. I am a fan of metal, indie, and many other genres of music. My life experiences, music, and nature all play a role in the art I create and the themes I choose to explore.


Thank you for taking this journey through my art with me, and I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoyed making it!
