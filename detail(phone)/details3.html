Design Details

Fisheye Studio


This project tasked me to create a business and effective advertising. The project required the creation of a logo, brand identity guide, mockups, and three unique advertisements. This business is a camera studio that targets young people with a passion for photography and a budget, and allows them to rent studio space and equipment. This work gave me the chance to experiment with complementary color palettes, organic shapes and odd perspectives. The fish purposefully attempts to capture the “fisheye angle” that some cameras have, creating a fun play-on-words and an eye-catching logo.
