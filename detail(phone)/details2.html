Design Details

The Shark Cove


This project was a non-profit organization that I created to learn effective branding and application. The project required the creation of a logo, brand identity, as well as mockups of the logo on objects. The organization focuses on preserving and maintaining shark sanctuaries to keep shark populations from decreasing. This work uses oceanic colors, symmetry, and shapes to make a simple yet effective point. The Shark Cove concept was made in Adobe Illustrator and then finalized in Adobe InDesign. Researching and creating this organization meant a lot to me, as I closely follow climate activism and care deeply for animals, especially sharks. I continue to let nature inspire my work, and have since worked on more shark-related ideas.
