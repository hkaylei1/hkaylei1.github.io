Design Details

Printmaking


These are some of my works that do not revolve around graphic design. My favorite print is titled “Loss” and was done by etching a copper plate. This work allowed me to have creative freedom to represent a dichotomy of our choice; I chose to explore the dichotomy of growth and decay. The piece that shows the obvious loss of the lizard’s tale, and how it seems incomplete without it. However, the loss of the frog’s tail is a sign of its growth and is a positive factor in its life. This work represents how the loss of something impacts individuals differently, and it personally helped me work through a difficult time. My other works explore similar themes of loss, growth, and change through life events. This class was very much out of my comfort zone, and I enjoyed learning new mediums and exploring another part of my artistic side.
