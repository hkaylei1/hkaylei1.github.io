AIGA 2024 Poster


I created this poster to advertise the 2024 AIGA Design Virtual Conference. This conference occurs annually, each year with its own unique theme and talented speakers. The class theme in 2024 challenged me to create a poster that implemented design elements from a different decade, while also containing all relevant information. I created a poster replicating a concert poster from the 1950s, using similar colors, shapes, and layout. In addition, in place of the musician, I included a computer that signifies that the conference is virtual. The final piece is 24x36 inches, and was outside of my comfort zone, as I have never worked on a piece that large digitally. I am very proud of the outcome and the color palette I chose.
